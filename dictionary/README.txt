Description: this folder includes the dictionaries that were used in our studies. 

File list:
    - dictionary1.csv: the dictionary used in pilot study 1.
    - dictionary2.csv: the dictionary used in pilot study 2, added words mentioned by participants as indicator for female/male applicants.
    - dictionary3.csv: the dictionary used in pilot study 3 and full study, added words mentioned by participants as indicator for female/male applicants, removed words that were perceived inconsistently with our dictionary, added a competitive category which include words not in our dictionary previouly but were mentioned by participants as indicators of competitiveness.
